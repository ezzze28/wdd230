Target Audience

Our target audience is primarily adults between the ages of 25-55 who are interested in networking with other businesses, improving their business knowledge, and growing their business in the local area. These individuals are typically business owners, entrepreneurs, or professionals who are looking to expand their network and stay informed about industry trends and news.

Personas

Persona 1: Name: Sarah Age: 35 Education: Bachelor's Degree Income: $60,000/year Family Status: Married with 2 kids Experience with the topic: 5 years in business as a small business owner Accessing the site: On her laptop during work hours

Persona 2: Name: John Age: 45 Education: Master's Degree Income: $100,000/year Family Status: Divorced, no kids Experience with the topic: 10 years as a business owner Accessing the site: On his smartphone during his daily commute.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenario 1: A local business owner is looking for information on how to apply for a business license in the area. They visit our site to find a step-by-step guide on the process and any necessary forms.

Scenario 2: A professional in the area is interested in attending a workshop on marketing strategies for small businesses. They visit our site to find information on upcoming workshops and register for them.

Scenario 3: A new resident in the area is looking for information on local businesses and services. They visit our site to find a directory of local businesses in various industries.

Scenario 4: A small business owner is looking for a space to rent for their business. They visit our site to find a list of available commercial properties in the area.

Scenario 5: A business owner needs to hire new employees but is having trouble finding qualified candidates. They visit our site to find job listings and post their own job openings.
