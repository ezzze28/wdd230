Target Audience

As London is a big city, the scope of the site is also big, but specific. This site wants to be capable of satisfy the needs of information from active merchants to small entrepreneurs that want to start their path. Also external agents like tourist and businessmen that want to come to the city for new experiences and opportunities.

Personas

Persona 1: A small entrepreneur person who wants to increase their business sales and build a strong relationship with the local businesses.

Persona 2: A father that is in needs to have a fresh start in a new place and is looking for a place where the economy is actively growing.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenario 1: University student wants to learn more about the benefits of starting a local business

Scenario 2: A family wants to know this year food festival dates.

Scenario 3: An enterprise need some third-party services from a gobernment associate.

Scenario 4: The owner of a growing company wants to register his trademark.

Scenario 5: An ngo wants to spread news about a charity event.
