Purpose

The purpose of Bountiful Foods is to provide the local population and coastal tourists with fresh, healthy, and organic food options. They aim to promote healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience through their storefront in Carlsbad, California, as well as their fleet of food trucks covering the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.
