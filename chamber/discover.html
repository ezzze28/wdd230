Favorites places for our tourists

Discover South Beach

Discover the world-famous South Beach, known for its stunning beaches, iconic Art Deco architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Explore the best of Miami's entertainment and relaxation hub.

Art Deco Historic District

Immerse yourself in the charm of Miami's Art Deco Historic District, featuring beautifully preserved pastel-colored buildings that transport you back in time.

Vizcaya Museum and Gardens

Experience the opulence of Vizcaya, a grand Italian Renaissance-style villa surrounded by lush gardens, showcasing the city's rich cultural heritage.

Wynwood Walls

The Wynwood Walls are an outdoor art gallery that showcases an ever-changing collection of colorful and innovative street art. It's a vibrant hub for contemporary urban art and a must-visit for art enthusiasts.

Little Havana

Immerse yourself in Cuban culture and traditions at Little Havana, where you can savor delicious Cuban cuisine, listen to live Latin music, and watch skilled salsa dancers perform in the streets.

Everglades National Park

Just a short drive from Miami, the Everglades National Park is a vast and unique ecosystem known for its swamplands, wildlife, and airboat tours. It's a perfect destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.
